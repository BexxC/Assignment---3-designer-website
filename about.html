Maguy Lee born on May 10, 1965, in Taichung City, Taiwan, she was raised by her parents Cheng Lee and Su Jen Lin, who both worked as chemistry professors at Taipei Medical University (TMU). She grew up with one younger brother, Yangzhe Li. Throughout her life, she has found joy in various forms of art, particularly drawing, of dolls growing up. With that, she pursued fashion design. For over 30 years until she moved to Canada, where she became an art teacher, with her own private studio. She developed a deep appreciation for nature, especially trees. The trajectory of her life has taken her across continents, from her homeland in Taiwan, including Taichung City and Taipei City, to France, where she resided in Angers and Paris for her studies, and finally to Canada with her family, with stays in Mississauga before moving to Richmond in 2010. This diverse array of experiences has undoubtedly enriched her perspective and creativity, shaping her into the individual she is today.

Maguy embarked on her artistic journey at Taibei Private Art High School Art (泰北高中美工科) in Taiwan before entering the workforce straight out of high school. At the age of 26, she further honed her skills at ESMOD International Fashion Design et Business school in France. Throughout her career, she has participated in numerous exhibitions and events, including the TCCS exhibitions in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2022, and 2024, showcasing collaborative works between teachers and students. Additionally, she has contributed her talents to the Taiwanese Canadian Cultural Festival from 2018 to 2024 as a T-shirt designer for volunteer designs, as well as creating posters and ads to promote the events. Since 2022, she has been actively involved in volunteer art teaching for the elderly at TCCS art gallery, displaying her commitment to sharing her passion for art with others. Her dedication and expertise led her to serve as the Art Director at TCCS from 2019 to 2024. With over 20 years of experience, she has also established her own fashion company, Maguy Fashion Group, leaving an indelible mark on the fashion industry.
